Project Title

Barangay Management System

Project Description

The Barangay Management System provides a modern digital solution that streamlines how barangay offices manage records, services, and transactions. By consolidating processes into a single, organized platform, the system reduces paperwork, minimizes the risk of lost files, and enables faster, more efficient service for residents.

The project aims to make barangay services faster, more accurate, and easier to access. Staff can efficiently manage resident information, issue documents, track requests, and maintain records with greater confidence and reliability.

This system serves barangay staff, officials, and residents seeking quicker and more convenient public service transactions.

Barangay Records
What It Can Do

Key Features & Functionalities

Resident Record Management

Store and manage resident profiles, household information, and demographic details.

Certificate & Document Issuance

Generate barangay clearance, certificate of residency, indigency certificate, and other official forms.

Request Tracking

Monitor the status of resident requests and document processing in real time.

Blotter & Incident Recording

Keep records of complaints, incidents, and barangay blotter reports.

User Account Management

Secure login access for administrators and authorized barangay personnel.

Search & Filter Functions

Quickly locate resident records, requests, and reports with powerful filters.

Report Generation

Produce organized reports for barangay activities, resident statistics, and transaction history.

Dashboard Overview

Display summaries of recent requests, issued documents, and barangay activities at a glance.
